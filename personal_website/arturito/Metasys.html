Foundations
While working at the NASA Michoud Assembly Facility, I interacted with the Johnson Controls family of controllers. As well as Johnson Controls Metasys software. I really enjoyed seeing how these devices worked and learning how facilities are networked into a centrally controllable site.
Physical Devices
There are 3 main devices in the Johnson networking solution. The FEC or Field Equipment Controller is a device level controller which essentially gathers all sensor output from a given assembly or machine. A modern unit is pictured left below, and a slightly older unit pictured right.
Graphics
I worked on or created ~30 graphics within the JCI Metasys agent used to monitor machinery and various assemblies throughout the facility. These graphics are facilitated by a central server,and are rendered to stationary personel to monitor and control in coordination with technicians in the field.
