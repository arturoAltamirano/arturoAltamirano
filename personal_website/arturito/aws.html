Review4U
Review4U was my senior capstone project, and is the main reason I decides to return to school and pursue a degree in Computer Science. It was a group project shared between me and other extremely gifted students. While I took on a majority of the development, their contributions to the project deserve mention. Essentially, Review4U is a Flask Web App which call the OpenAI Chat API to respond to a basic text prompt detailing a customers time at a resturant. These inputs, gathered from the user, are then compiled into a text blob to be posted to a review board like Yelp.
Outcomes
This was my first in depth development experience, I designed, developed, and tested this thing from nothing. No real guidance was given outside of an idea and a few technologies to consider. I started from a blank IDE, and eventually had a very crude Tkinter executable. I was told this needed to be accesible anywhere on the internet, so I decided to turn it into a web app. Eventually, our Flask Application was developed. Our group was awarded first overall among all other groups of graduating seniors, and I had discovered my love for development and creation.
